Privacy Policy

v2.0 · Effective 2025
Last Updated: July 5, 2026
Your privacy matters. We are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Introduction

Blood Donor BD (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, including our website and mobile application.

By using Blood Donor BD, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Identification: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, blood group, and location (district/city).
  • Account Credentials: Password (hashed and secured) and authentication data.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, request history, donor interactions, and time stamps.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for analytics and security.
  • Health Information: Only basic health self-declarations (e.g., eligibility to donate) – we do not store sensitive medical records.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Matching & Communication: To connect blood seekers with compatible donors and facilitate emergency requests.
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your profile, verify identity, and manage your preferences.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage trends, enhance user experience, and develop new features.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To monitor suspicious activity, enforce our Terms, and protect users.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

  • With Other Users: When you respond to a request or contact a donor, your phone number and basic profile info (name, blood group) may be shared to facilitate the transaction.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party services (Firebase hosting, analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred with prior notice.

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to user data, and they are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Storage: Data is stored in secure Firebase environments with industry-standard safeguards.
  • User Responsibility: You are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential. Notify us immediately of any breach.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: You can access and update your profile information at any time through your account settings.
  • Deletion: You may request account deletion by contacting us. We will remove your data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Opt-Out: You can choose to hide your profile from public search or deactivate your donor status at any time.
  • Communication Preferences: You can unsubscribe from non-essential notifications via your settings or the “unsubscribe” link in emails.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and remember preferences. You can control cookie settings in your browser, but disabling cookies may affect some functionality.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and basic platform operation.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the platform (via Firebase Analytics).
  • Third-Party Cookies: We may use embedded content (e.g., maps) that sets their own cookies.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. After account deletion, we will anonymize or delete your data within 30 days, except for data we are legally obligated to retain.

9. Children’s Privacy

Blood Donor BD is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

10. International Users

Our platform is operated from Bangladesh. If you are accessing from outside Bangladesh, please be aware that your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Bangladesh or other countries where our service providers are located. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on the platform. The “Last Updated” date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
